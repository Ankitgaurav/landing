Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful?

Modern RCT is done under local anaesthesia using rotary instruments — most patients feel no pain. You can return to normal activities the same day. Mild sensitivity for 1-2 days after is normal and resolves quickly.

How long does a dental implant take?

Implant surgery takes about 1 hour. After 2-3 months of healing (osseointegration), the final crown is placed. Total process takes 3-4 months. Temporary solutions may be available in some cases.

What is the cost of braces in Motihari?

At COSMODENTZ, a full braces (orthodontic) treatment package starts from ₹20,000. The exact amount depends on case complexity, type of braces chosen and duration. A personalised quote is given after an orthodontic consultation.

At what age should children visit a dentist?

The first dental visit should ideally be when the first milk tooth appears (around 6 months). Regular visits every 6 months are recommended from age 2. Early visits help identify decay and habits that affect tooth alignment.

What is PRP hair treatment?

Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy involves drawing a small amount of blood, processing it to concentrate growth factors, and injecting it into the scalp to stimulate hair follicles. 3-4 sessions are recommended, typically 4-6 weeks apart. PRP facials use the same technique for skin rejuvenation.
