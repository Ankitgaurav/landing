About Our Clinic

Welcome to COSMODENTZ Dental Clinic

At COSMODENTZ, we are committed to providing exceptional dental care with a personal touch. Led by Dr. Pradeep Kumar Jayswal, our clinic combines years of experience with modern dental technology to deliver the best possible outcomes for our patients.

We believe in creating a comfortable, anxiety-free environment where patients feel valued and cared for. From routine check-ups to advanced cosmetic procedures, we handle every case with precision, care, and professionalism.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Motihari is equipped with the latest dental equipment, ensuring you receive world-class treatment right in your neighborhood.
